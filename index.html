Hello

I am a data analyst at the Puget Sound Regional Council who works with land use and travel forecast modellers. I studied Geographic Information Systems (GIS), but over the years my interests focused less on GIS and more on automating data wrangling and visualization.

I am an R programmer trying to learn Python and often debating whether or not I should use Shiny or Dash. When not in front of a computer, you can find me in a dance studio or knitting.
